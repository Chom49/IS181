Education Journey


From St. Mary's Junior School in 2009 to Baobab Secondary School and now the University of Dar es Salaam, my educational journey has been transformative. Each milestone has shaped my understanding and fueled my passion for knowledge. Transitioning from foundational principles to intricate concepts, I've grown exponentially. This evolution instills in me a deep appreciation for learning and a drive to push boundaries. As I navigate university life pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, I carry with me invaluable lessons, shaping my path toward a future brimming with possibilities in the realm of technology.

Sponsership & Career Goals

My father generously funds my education, alleviating financial worries and empowering me to focus on my studies. His belief in education fuels my determination for success. I aspire to excel as a software developer and network administrator, utilizing my computer science expertise to innovate and optimize network systems. Driven by passion and a commitment to excellence, I aim to leave a lasting impact in these fields, supported by my family every step of the way.
